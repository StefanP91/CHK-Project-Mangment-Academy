Enrollment Requirements:

  • English is not required – The program is taught in Albanian. However, having English skills can improve your chances of getting hired, especially in the tech industry.
  • Laptop required – You must have a laptop or computer to attend classes, complete assignments, and participate in program activities.
  • No prior knowledge or experience needed – All programs start from the basics and are beginner-friendly.
  • Motivation is a must – A strong drive to learn and grow is essential for success in our programs.
  • U.S. Diploma Program – To enroll in the U.S. Diploma track, you must apply with a notarized copy of your high school diploma translated into English.

At Creative Hub, we offer four types of certifications:

  1. Creative Hub Certificate – Awarded upon completion of any of our academy programs.
  2. SHRM-Accredited Certificate – Available through our HR Academy; students also earn SHRM Professional Development Credits (PDCs) for recertification.
  3. American Diploma – Recognized by all public and private institutions in the United States.
  4. EU Diploma Supplement – Provided for international recognition within the European education framework.

Creative Hub is the only Academy in Kosovo and Macedonia accredited by the U.S.
Department of Education.

All Creative Hub Academy programs are accredited to be taught in the Albanian language, and students are eligible to receive a U.S. diploma. Creative Hub Academy is an accredited institution by the U.S. Department of Education, with offices in Miami, Pristina, and Skopje.
